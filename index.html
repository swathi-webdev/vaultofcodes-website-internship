FAQs

Top Queries

Who is this session for?

This session is for students, freshers, and beginners interested in Web Development, Python, or AI tools, and looking for practical training with internship opportunities.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, you will receive a certificate upon successful completion of the internship program.

Do I need any prior coding experience?

No, our programs are designed for beginners. We start from the basics and gradually build up your skills.

What are the program durations and schedules?

Program durations vary from 1-3 months depending on the course. We offer flexible timing with both weekday and weekend batches.

What if I face any technical problem or query?

We have a dedicated support team available 24/7 to help you with any technical issues or queries you might have.
